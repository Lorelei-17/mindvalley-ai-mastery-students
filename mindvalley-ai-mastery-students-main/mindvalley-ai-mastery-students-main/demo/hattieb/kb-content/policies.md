# Hattie B's Hot Chicken - Business Policies

## Reservation Policy

**Walk-In Only**: Hattie B's operates on a strict first-come, first-served basis with no advance reservations accepted for dine-in service. This reflects the brand's fast-casual positioning and high-volume operational model.

**Large Groups (8+ People)**:
- Cannot guarantee adjacent seating
- Unable to reserve specific restaurant areas
- Alternative: Catering pickup or delivery recommended for groups

**Private Dining Exceptions**:
- Available at select locations only:
  - Nashville Midtown (112 19th Ave S) - capacity up to 50 people
  - Atlanta West Midtown (711 10th St NW) - capacity up to 40 people
- Requires advance notice with date, time, and expected headcount
- Subject to availability confirmation

## Online Ordering Policy

**Advance Ordering**: Up to 2 weeks in advance booking available

**ASAP Limitations**: May not be available during high demand periods

**Time Slots**: System updates availability every 15-20 minutes

**Off-Premises Requirement**: All online orders must be consumed off-site (line-skip service for pickup only, not for dine-in)

**Order Modifications**: Limited ability to change orders after placement

## Refund & Complaint Handling

**Primary Contact Methods**:
- Contact Us Form (preferred method for complaint resolution)
- Email: info@hattieb.com for general issues
- Email: events@hattieb.com for catering issues
- Phone Support: Lines open at 11 AM daily (throttled during peak periods)

**Response Standards**:
- Hospitality Manager contact within one business day of complaint submission
- Regional Supervisor escalation available for unresolved issues
- All incidents tracked for pattern analysis
- Post-resolution satisfaction verification

**Service Recovery**: Multiple staff levels empowered for compensation and service recovery decisions

## Catering Policies

**Small Group Orders (Under 15 people)**:
- Ordering Method: Group Order feature through mobile app
- Notice Required: 24 hours minimum advance notice
- Service Options: Pickup or delivery available
- Restaurant Coordination: Direct contact with individual locations

**Large Catering Orders (15+ people)**:
- Dedicated Catering Team handles these orders
- Advance Notice: 48-72 hours recommended for average orders
- Customization: Event-specific menu planning and coordination
- Volume Pricing: Structured pricing based on guest count

**Catering Delivery Requirements**:
- Minimum Order: $300 food and beverage before taxes and fees
- Service Capacity: Typically accommodates 20-25 people
- Geographic Coverage: Delivery fees range $20-50 based on distance
- Nashville Kitchen: $25 flat fee within 10-mile radius from 450 Atlas Drive catering kitchen

**Full-Service Catering**:
- Minimum Order: $2,500 food and beverage requirement
- Guest Capacity: Typically serves 100+ people
- Advance Notice: 2 weeks minimum required
- Staff Provision: Full-service team available at $25/hour per staff member

**Food Truck Requirements**:
- Space Needs: 50-foot flat surface for setup
- Power Requirements: 100 amps minimum or provided generator
- Service Options: Multiple heat level stations and buffet configurations

## Gift Card Terms

**Available Formats**:
- Physical cards available at all restaurant locations
- Digital cards available via website
- Corporate bulk purchasing available
- Custom denominations available

**CRITICAL RESTRICTION**: Gift cards are NOT redeemable at:
- Las Vegas location (The LINQ)
- BNA Airport location

**Standard Redemption Locations**: All traditional Hattie B's restaurant locations, online ordering systems (excluding third-party platforms), dine-in, take-out, and direct online orders

**Usage Policies**:
- Partial Use: Cards can be used partially with remaining balance tracked
- Balance Inquiry: Check balance feature available on website
- No Expiration: Cards maintain value indefinitely
- Multiple Payment: Can be combined with other payment methods
- Lost/Stolen Cards: No replacement available - treated like cash
- Damaged Cards: Case-by-case evaluation for replacement

## COOP Club Loyalty Program Policies

**Point System**:
- Earning Rate: 10 points per $1 spent on qualifying purchases
- Redemption Threshold: 1000 points automatically converts to $10 reward
- Point Expiration: 12 months from earning date with 3-month advance warnings
- Automatic Processing: Points appear within moments to 3 business days depending on payment processor

**Critical Restrictions**:
- Third-Party Exclusion: CANNOT use Coop Club rewards with DoorDash orders
- Cart-Based Availability: Some rewards only available based on current cart contents
- Multiple Reward Usage: Customers can stack multiple rewards in single transaction

**Account Management**:
- Registration: Mobile app and website
- Receipt Upload: Manual entry available for cash purchases
- Account Recovery: Customer service assistance for login issues

## Large Party Guidance

For groups of 15+ people, Hattie B's strongly recommends using catering services rather than attempting dine-in service due to:
- No reservation system
- Inability to guarantee group seating
- Potential extended wait times during peak periods
- Better experience through dedicated catering service

## Wait Time Policy

**Peak Period Communication**:
- Staff provides regular wait time updates
- Extended wait times can exceed one hour during busy periods
- Water service provided to customers waiting in outdoor lines
- Some locations feature entertainment during wait periods
- Online ordering promoted as alternative for pickup service

**Weather-Related Closures**: Communicated proactively via social media
