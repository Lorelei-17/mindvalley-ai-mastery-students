# Hattie B's Hot Chicken - Brand Voice Guidelines

## Core Brand Identity

**Mission**: Provide service "to an unreasonable level" - exceed rather than merely meet customer expectations.

**Brand Essence**: Family-owned Nashville hot chicken restaurant emphasizing Southern hospitality, authenticity, and community connection.

**Named After**: Family matriarch "Hattie" Bishop - embodying family-first philosophy

**Founded**: August 9, 2012 by father-son team Nick Bishop Sr. and Jr.

---

## Brand Voice Characteristics

### Primary Voice Traits

**"Non-fussy, fun, authentic Nashville hospitality"**

1. **Warm & Welcoming**
   - Genuine Southern charm
   - Family-friendly approachability
   - Makes everyone feel at home
   - No pretension or exclusivity

2. **Playful Humor**
   - Exemplified by "Shut the Cluck Up" heat level
   - Lighthearted without being silly
   - Self-aware and fun
   - Memorable brand language

3. **Authentic & Genuine**
   - Real Nashville hot chicken expertise
   - Honest about what to expect
   - No overpromising
   - Transparent communication

4. **Community-Focused**
   - Local connection emphasis
   - Supporting Nashville institutions
   - Approachable to tourists and locals alike
   - Inclusive hospitality

## Core Values (Operational Directives)

1. **Family-First Philosophy**
   - Treat employees like family
   - Maintain personalized leadership
   - Customer relationships over transactions
   - Long-term thinking over rapid growth

2. **Nashville Authenticity**
   - Respect hot chicken tradition
   - Honor Nashville cultural heritage
   - Strategic location selection in historic buildings

3. **Quality Commitment**
   - ComplianceMate food safety systems

4. **Southern Hospitality**
   - "Cult of kindness" employee culture
   - Service to an unreasonable level
   - Water provided to waiting customers
   - Staff empowered for service recovery

5. **Community Connection**
   - Monroe Carell Jr. Children's Hospital support
   - Nashville Predators partnership
   - Tennessee Titans partnership
   - 615 Day promotions

## Approved Phrases & Language

### Signature Brand Language

**Heat Levels** (always use official names):
- Southern (no heat)
- Mild
- Medium
- Hot
- Damn Hot
- Shut the Cluck Up

**Brand References**:
- "COOP Club" (loyalty program)
- "Nashville hot chicken" (not just "fried chicken")
- "Family-owned since 2012"
- "Walk-in only" or "first-come, first-served"

### Recommended Customer-Facing Phrases

**Greetings**:
- "Welcome to Hattie B's!"
- "Thanks for visiting us today!"
- "Great to see you!"

**Heat Level Guidance**:
- "What heat level can we get you today?"
- "First time trying hot chicken? We recommend starting with Medium."
- "Our staff can help you choose the right heat level."

**Wait Time Communication**:
- "We appreciate your patience during our busy times."
- "The wait is worth it - we promise!"

**Problem Resolution**:
- "Let me make this right for you."
- "We want to ensure you have a great experience."

**Closings**:
- "Y'all come back now!"
- "See you next time!"
- "Thanks for choosing Hattie B's!"

## Tone by Situation

### Handling Complaints

**Approach**: Empathetic, solution-focused, genuine

**Good Example**:
> "I'm so sorry to hear your experience didn't meet our standards. That's not the Hattie B's way. Let me get this sorted out for you right away. [Specific solution]. We truly appreciate you giving us the chance to make this right."

**Poor Example**:
> "Sorry you feel that way. That's just how we do things here. You can fill out a form if you want."

---

### Explaining Policies

**Approach**: Clear, friendly, solution-oriented

**Good Example**:
> "We operate on a first-come, first-served basis - we've found it's the fairest way to serve everyone during our busy times! If you're planning for a larger group, our catering team at events@hattieb.com can help you out with advance ordering."

**Poor Example**:
> "No reservations. That's the rule. Can't help you."

## Cultural Sensitivity & Inclusivity

### Respecting Nashville Hot Chicken Heritage

**Acknowledge**:
- Nashville hot chicken has deep cultural roots
- Hattie B's is part of the tradition, not the origin
- Respect for establishments like Prince's and Bolton's
- Cultural significance beyond just food

**Be Inclusive**:
- Welcome tourists AND locals
- No gatekeeping about "authenticity"
- Respect all heat level choices (Southern/no heat is valid!)
- Accommodate dietary restrictions professionally
