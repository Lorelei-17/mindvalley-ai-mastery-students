# Hattie B's Hot Chicken - Frequently Asked Questions

## Wait Times

### How long are typical wait times?

Wait times vary significantly by location and time:

**Tourist/High-Traffic Locations** (Lower Broad, Midtown):
- Peak hours (lunch 11:30 AM-2 PM, dinner 6-9 PM): 60-120 minutes
- Weekend peak times can extend "around the block"
- Friday-Sunday are busiest days

**Suburban Locations** (Franklin, West Nashville):
- Peak hours: 20-45 minutes
- Generally shorter waits than downtown locations

**Airport Location (BNA)**:
- Typical wait: 5-15 minutes
- Fastest service due to traveler traffic patterns

**Business District Locations**:
- Lunch rush: 15-45 minutes
- Evening service: 30-45 minutes

### What are the best times to visit?

**Weekday afternoons (2-5 PM)** consistently offer the shortest wait times at most locations.

**Avoid these peak periods**:
- Friday-Saturday evenings (6 PM-10 PM) - longest waits
- Lunch rush (11:30 AM-2 PM) any day
- Special events (CMA week, music festivals in Nashville, State Fair/Cowboys games in Dallas, SXSW/ACL in Austin)

**Airport exception**: Wait times are consistently low throughout operating hours.

## Ordering Ahead

### Can I order ahead to skip the line?

**Yes** - Multiple options available:

**Mobile App/Website Ordering**:
- Order up to 2 weeks in advance
- Use dedicated pickup counter (skip the dine-in line)
- Time slots update every 15-20 minutes
- IMPORTANT: All online orders must be consumed off-site (no dine-in with online orders)

**ASAP Ordering**:
- May not be available during high-demand periods
- System will show available pickup times

**For Groups Under 15**:
- Use "Group Order" feature in mobile app
- Requires 24 hours minimum notice

### Can I make a reservation for dine-in?

**No** - Hattie B's operates strictly first-come, first-served with no reservations.

**Exception**: Private dining rooms available at Nashville Midtown (up to 50 people) and Atlanta West Midtown (up to 40 people) - requires advance booking.

**For large groups**: Catering pickup or delivery recommended over attempting dine-in service.

## Allergen & Dietary Information

### Do you have gluten-free options?

**Yes** - Several options available:

**Gluten-Free Proteins**:
- Grilled chicken tenders (available at most locations, NOT Las Vegas)
- "Naked" chicken - fried without breading
- Gluten-free spice blends available upon request

**Gluten-Free Sides**:
- Multiple options clearly marked on menu
- Staff trained to identify safe items

**Important Notes**:
- Fries share fryer space (potential cross-contamination)
- Separate preparation protocols available
- Staff can communicate special preparation requests to kitchen

### What allergens should I be aware of?

**Critical Allergen Warnings**:
- **Garlic/Onion**: High cross-contamination risk in spice blends
- **Nuts**: No nuts used in recipes, but cannot guarantee vendor facility safety
- **Soy and Egg**: Present in breader and various sides

**Allergen Support**:
- Menu items clearly marked for common allergens
- Trained staff can provide detailed information
- Call ahead for severe allergies

## COOP Club Loyalty Program

### How does the COOP Club work?

**Earning Points**:
- Earn 10 points per $1 spent on qualifying purchases
- Points appear within moments to 3 business days (depending on payment processor)
- Cash purchases: Upload receipt manually through app

**Redeeming Points**:
- 1000 points = $10 reward (automatically converted)
- Redeem via QR code in app or account lookup by staff
- Can stack multiple rewards in single transaction
- Some rewards are cart-based (only available with certain items)

**Point Expiration**:
- Points expire 12 months from earning date
- 3-month advance warning before expiration
- Check "Expiring Points" tab in app

**IMPORTANT RESTRICTION**: CANNOT use COOP Club rewards with DoorDash or third-party delivery orders

### How do I check my COOP Club balance?

- **Mobile App**: View in Rewards section (real-time updates)
- **Website**: Log in to account
- **In-Store**: Staff can look up by phone number or email

### I paid with cash - how do I get points?

Upload your receipt through the COOP Club mobile app. Points will be manually credited to your account.

## Catering

### Do you offer catering?

**Yes** - Multiple tiers available:

**Small Groups (Under 15 people)**:
- Use mobile app "Group Order" feature
- 24 hours minimum notice
- Pickup or delivery available

**Large Groups (15+ people)**:
- Dedicated catering team
- Email: events@hattieb.com
- 48-72 hours recommended notice
- Custom menu planning available

**Full-Service Events (100+ people)**:
- $2,500 minimum food and beverage
- 2 weeks minimum notice
- Staff available at $25/hour per person
- Food truck options available

### What are catering minimums and fees?

**Delivery Minimums**:
- $300 food and beverage before taxes/fees (serves 20-25 people)
- Nashville: $25 flat fee within 10-mile radius of catering kitchen
- Outside radius: $20-50 based on distance

**Full-Service Minimum**: $2,500 (typically serves 100+ people)

**Food Truck Requirements**:
- 50-foot flat surface for setup
- 100 amps power minimum (or provided generator)

## Location-Specific Questions

### Can I eat at the restaurant if I order online?

**No** - All online orders are for off-premises consumption only. Online orders use the dedicated pickup counter and cannot be consumed in the dining room.

For dine-in service, you must order at the counter after arriving.

### Why can't I use my gift card in Las Vegas or at the airport?

These locations have different operating agreements due to their casino and airport partnerships. This restriction is clearly communicated at time of gift card purchase.

### Does the airport location require going through security?

**Yes** - The BNA Airport location is inside Concourse C, which requires clearing airport security to access.
