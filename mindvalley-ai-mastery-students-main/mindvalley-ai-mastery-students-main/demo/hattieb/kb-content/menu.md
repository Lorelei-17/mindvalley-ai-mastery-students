# Hattie B's Hot Chicken - Menu & Products

## Heat Levels

Hattie B's signature heat level system ranges from no heat to extreme spice. Each level is carefully crafted with authentic Nashville hot chicken preparation methods.

**Six Heat Levels**:

1. **Southern** (No Heat)
   - Perfect for those who want the flavor without any spice
   - Family-friendly option
   - All the Nashville hot chicken experience, zero heat

2. **Mild**
   - Gentle introduction to Nashville hot chicken
   - Noticeable warmth without intense burn
   - Great for first-timers

3. **Medium**
   - Classic Nashville hot chicken heat
   - Balanced spice that enhances flavor
   - Most popular choice for regular customers

4. **Hot**
   - Significant heat level
   - For spice enthusiasts
   - Authentic Nashville intensity

5. **Damn Hot**
   - Serious heat territory
   - Not for beginners
   - Challenge-level spice

6. **Shut the Cluck Up**
   - Extreme heat level
   - Hattie B's signature hottest offering
   - Featured in merchandise and brand identity
   - For extreme heat seekers only

## Dietary Accommodations

### Gluten-Free Options

**Available Preparations**:
- **Grilled Chicken Tenders**: Available at most locations (NOT Las Vegas)
- **Naked Chicken**: Fried without breading for gluten-sensitive customers
- **Gluten-Free Spice Blends**: Available upon request
- **Multiple Sides**: Clearly marked on menu

**Important Notes**:
- Separate preparation protocols for allergen-sensitive customers
- Staff trained to identify safe menu items
- Cross-contamination: Fries share fryer space
- Kitchen communication: Special preparation requests communicated to cooking staff

### Allergen Information

**Critical Allergen Warnings**:
- **Garlic/Onion Sensitivity**: High cross-contamination risk in spice blends
- **Nut-Free Status**: No nuts used in recipes, but cannot guarantee vendor facility safety
- **Soy and Egg Content**: Present in breader and various sides

**Allergen Support**:
- Menu marking for common allergens
- Trained staff can provide detailed allergen information
- Special preparation requests accommodated when possible
- Customers with severe allergies encouraged to call ahead

### Special Dietary Modifications

**Available Modifications**:
- Naked Chicken (fried without breading)
- Grilled options (most locations)
- Sauce alternatives (gluten-free spice blends)
- Side modifications (dairy-free and gluten-free options)

**Staff Support**:
- Comprehensive allergen knowledge training
- Kitchen communication protocols for special requests
- Staff can identify safe menu items for various dietary restrictions
