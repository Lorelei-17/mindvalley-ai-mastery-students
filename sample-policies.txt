# Company Policies

## Shipping Policy

### Domestic Shipping
- Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days ($5.99)
- Express Shipping: 2 business days ($12.99)
- Overnight Shipping: Next business day ($24.99)
- Free shipping on orders over $50

### International Shipping
- Currently available to Canada only
- Delivery: 7-10 business days
- Customs fees are the responsibility of the customer

### Shipping Restrictions
- We do not ship to PO boxes
- We do not ship to military APO/FPO addresses
- Orders placed after 2pm EST ship the next business day

## Return & Refund Policy

### Return Window
- 30 days from date of delivery
- Items must be in original, unused condition
- Original packaging required

### Refund Process
1. Contact customer service for RMA number
2. Ship item back within 7 days of receiving RMA
3. Refund processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item
4. Refund issued to original payment method

### Non-Returnable Items
- Opened food or personal care items
- Custom or personalized products
- Sale items marked "Final Sale"
- Digital products or downloads

### Return Shipping Costs
- Defective items: We pay return shipping
- Change of mind: Customer pays return shipping
- Wrong item sent: We pay return shipping
